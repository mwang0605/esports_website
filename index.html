River Trail E-Sports

Over the decades video games have rapidly evolved from the simple game of Pong to the racing styles of Mario Cart, with the continuous evolution creativity is endless. Many think that video games are purely made for entertainment and that it would never be much more, however all over the world with the evolution of computer games, the e-sport community grows larger and larger by the second. Here at River Trail we believe in allowing a passage for students to enter this industry and succeced. Our staff have taken time out of their day to make this dream come true, and create this unique experience for the students.
